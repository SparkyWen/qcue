Delete your QCue account

You can permanently delete your QCue account and all associated data at any time.

In the app (fastest)

  1. Open QCue.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

This immediately and irreversibly deletes your account, your captures, your generated wiki, your stored provider keys, and all synced data on our servers.

If you no longer have the app

Email privacy@qcue.cn from the address you registered with and ask to delete your account. We will verify ownership and delete your account and data, typically within 30 days.

What is deleted: your account and email, all notes and transcripts, the generated wiki, stored AI provider keys, and synced data. Retention: nothing is retained after deletion except where required by law.

See also our Privacy Policy.
