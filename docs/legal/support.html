QCue Support

Need help with QCue? We're happy to assist.

Contact

Email support@qcue.cn (or privacy@qcue.cn for privacy and data requests). We aim to reply within a few business days.

Common questions

  • QCue needs an AI key. QCue is “bring your own key”: add your own provider key (OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepSeek, and more) under Settings to enable the AI features. Your notes are processed only by the provider you choose.
  • Voice notes. Microphone and speech recognition are used only when you dictate a note.
  • Delete your account. Settings → Account → Delete Account removes your account and all synced data. See Delete your account.
  • Privacy. See our Privacy Policy.
