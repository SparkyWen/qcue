QCue Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-18

QCue ("QCue", "we", "us") is a capture-first personal knowledge app: you jot fleeting ideas by text or voice, and a large language model (LLM) of your choosing distills them into a private markdown wiki. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. We designed QCue to keep your data minimal, private to you, and under your control.

In short: We collect only what the app needs to function. We do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, and we do not track you across other apps or websites or sell your data.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information — the email address you register with and a securely hashed password (or a one-time sign-in link). Used to create and secure your account.
  • Content you create — the notes, ideas, voice recordings and their transcripts, shared text/links, and the wiki pages QCue generates from them. This is your "second brain" and is stored on your device and, when you are signed in, synced to your private, tenant-isolated space on our servers.
  • Provider API keys you add (BYOK — "bring your own key") — if you choose to use an LLM or cloud speech provider, the API key you enter is stored encrypted. We never display it again in full and never write it to logs in plaintext.
  • Operational data — basic, non-advertising information needed to run the service (e.g., a per-account usage/cost ledger for the LLM calls you make, and standard server logs that may include IP address and timestamps for security and abuse-prevention).

2. How we use your information

  • To provide the core features: capture, sync, the generated wiki, recall/search, and the nightly consolidation ("Auto-Dream").
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To operate, maintain, debug, and protect the service against abuse.

We do not use your content to train our own models, and we do not use it for advertising.

3. Voice and speech

When you dictate a note, transcription is performed on your device wherever the operating system supports it. If on-device recognition is unavailable, or you opt into higher-accuracy cloud transcription, the audio may be sent to a cloud speech-to-text provider to produce the transcript. The app discloses this before recording. Only the resulting text transcript is stored as your note; the raw audio is not retained after transcription.

4. Location (optional, off by default)

QCue can optionally tag a capture with your precise location. This feature is off by default and only activated after you explicitly enable "Tag captures with location" in Settings. When enabled, the app reads your device's GPS coordinates at the moment you make a capture and stores the latitude/longitude with that capture so you have context for where the idea occurred. Location is never collected in the background and is never polled at any time other than the instant you actively submit a capture. The coordinates are stored with your note in your private vault and are used solely for your own context — they are not used for advertising, shared with third parties (other than the infrastructure and BYOK providers that store and process your content generally), or transmitted unless you sync your captures to our servers.

5. Third-party LLM and speech providers (BYOK)

QCue's intelligence is powered by providers you select and connect with your own API key (for example OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepSeek, or any compatible provider). To generate or recall content, the relevant text from your notes is sent to the provider you configured, using your key, so it can return a result. That content is processed by the third-party provider under their terms and privacy policy, which govern how they handle it. We act only as the conduit that uses your key on your behalf; please review your chosen provider's privacy practices.

6. Storage, security, and isolation

  • All network traffic uses HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit.
  • API keys are encrypted at rest using authenticated encryption (AES-GCM) and, on device, the operating-system secure enclave/Keychain; biometric unlock can be required before a key is read.
  • On our servers, each account's data is isolated at the database level (row-level security), so one account cannot access another's data.
  • Captured ideas are stored locally first so that nothing is lost even when you are offline.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we apply industry-standard safeguards.

7. Data retention and deletion

Your content is kept until you delete it or close your account. You can delete individual items in the app, and you can delete your account from within the app; deleting your account removes your synced content and provider keys from our active systems. Backups and security logs are purged on a rolling schedule.

8. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only:

  • with the LLM/speech providers you choose to connect (see section 5);
  • with infrastructure providers that host and operate the service on our behalf, under confidentiality obligations; and
  • where required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users and the service.

9. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. If you are in the EEA or UK (GDPR) or California (CCPA/CPRA), these rights apply to you; we do not "sell" or "share" personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. To exercise any right, contact us using the details below.

10. Children

QCue is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction) and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

11. International transfers

QCue is operated from infrastructure that may be located in a different country from yours, and the providers you connect may process data in other countries. Where required, we rely on appropriate legal transfer mechanisms.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, by notice in the app.

13. Contact

Questions or requests regarding this policy or your data: privacy@qcue.cn.
