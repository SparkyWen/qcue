# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

QCue is a space for respectful, professional collaboration. Everyone is welcome regardless of
background, identity, or experience level.

We expect participants to:

- be respectful, considerate, and constructive;
- assume good faith and offer feedback kindly;
- accept disagreement gracefully and focus on the technical merits.

We do **not** tolerate:

- harassment, intimidation, or abuse of any kind;
- discrimination or demeaning comments based on personal characteristics;
- hostile, threatening, or persistently disruptive behavior;
- publishing others' private information without permission.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other project
communication — and to public conduct that affects the safety of the community.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported privately to **helios@sinox.ai**. Reports will be reviewed and handled
discreetly. Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warnings, removal of
contributions, or banning from project spaces.

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and are expected to apply them fairly and
consistently.
